Doc Scanner User Guide

Welcome to Doc Scanner! This tool is designed to help you write clearer, more precise, and highly professional technical documents. Think of it as your intelligent built-in editor that enforces industry-standard style guidelines instantly.

1. Getting Started

What does Doc Scanner do?

It reads your documents (.txt, .md, or .pdf) and identifies areas that could be improved, including:

  • Passive Voice: Makes your sentences more direct and active.
  • Complex Sentences: Breaks down long, confusing text.
  • Terminology: Ensures standard UI terms (e.g., using "Click" instead of "Click on").
  • Readability: Guarantees your document is accessible to your audience.

2. How to Scan a Document

  • Upload your file: Locate the Upload Document section on the left side of the dashboard.
  • Select a file: Click the area or simply drag and drop your document.
  • Analyze: Click the Upload & Analyze button.
  • Wait a moment: The scanner will process your document line by line and display the results in the reading pane on the right.

3. Understanding Your Results

Once analysis finishes, your document is split into interactive sentences in the Document Content & Feedback area.

Visual Indicators

Clean Text (Green/No highlights)
Sentences that are perfectly written and follow all rules!
Highlighted Text (Yellow/Orange)
Sentences with stylistic warnings or readability improvements.
Highlighted Text (Red)
Critical errors that violate strict style or formatting rules.

4. Fixing Issues

Doc Scanner doesn't just point out mistakes—it actively helps you resolve them!

  • Click the Issue Card: Whenever you see an issue in the right-hand pane, click directly on the issue card.
  • Read the Guidance: A detailed pop-up will explain exactly which rule was broken and why.
  • Make Your Correction: Use the provided rule rationale to understand the issue. Type your improved version into the text box and click Apply Correction to instantly update your document!

5. Exporting Your Work

When you're happy with your polished document, save your progress:

  • Look for the Export button at the top right of the Document Content area.
  • Click it to download your pristine, fully-corrected text file back to your computer.

6. Tips for Success

Pace Yourself

Don't get overwhelmed by a sea of red! Click through issues one by one.

You Are In Control

You don't have to accept every suggestion. Your judgement overrides the rules.

Use Dark Mode

Toggle the Dark Mode switch in the navbar to save your eyes during long sessions!

Happy Writing!
