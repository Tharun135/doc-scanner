# 📖 Doc Scanner: End User Guide

Welcome to **Doc Scanner**! ✨ This tool is designed to help you write clearer, more precise, and highly professional technical documents. It automatically scans your text and acts like a built-in editor, ensuring your writing follows industry-standard style guidelines.

This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started. 🚀

---

## 1. 🎯 Getting Started

### What does Doc Scanner do?
Doc Scanner reads your documents (like `.txt`, `.md`, or `.pdf`) and highlights sentences that could be improved. It looks for things like:
* ✒️ **Passive Voice:** Making your sentences more direct and active.
* 🧩 **Complex Sentences:** Breaking down long, confusing sentences.
* 📖 **Inconsistent Terminology:** Ensuring you use the correct UI terms (e.g., using "Click" instead of "Click on").
* 👁️ **Readability:** Making sure your document is easy for anyone to understand.

---

## 2. 📤 How to Scan a Document

1. **Upload your file:** On the main dashboard, look for the **Upload Document** section on the left side of the screen.
2. **Select a file:** Click the upload area or drag and drop your document into the box.
3. **Analyze:** Click the **Upload & Analyze** button. ⚡
4. **Wait a moment:** The scanner will read your document line by line. Once finished, your document text will appear in the main reading pane on the right.

---

## 3. 🔍 Understanding Your Results

Once the analysis is complete, you will see your document split into sentences in the large **Document Content & Feedback** area.

### Visual Indicators
* 🟢 **Clean Text (Green/No highlights):** Sentences that are perfectly written and follow all rules!
* 🟠 **Highlighted Text (Yellow/Orange/Red):** Sentences that need your attention. The color usually represents the severity of the issue (e.g., Red = Error, Yellow = Warning).

### The AI Assistant Icon
Next to any sentence that has an issue, you will see a small 🤖 **Robot Icon** (or a 🪄 **Magic Wand Icon** if there are multiple issues). 

---

## 4. 🛠️ Fixing Issues

Doc Scanner doesn't just tell you what's wrong—it helps you fix it!

1. **Click the Robot/Wand Icon:** Whenever you see an issue, click the icon next to that sentence.
2. **Read the Rule Guidance:** A pop-up window will appear showing you exactly which rule was broken. It will explain *why* it's an issue in simple terms. 🧠
3. **Apply the Correction:** 
   * Often, the tool will provide a perfectly rewritten version of your sentence. 
   * If you like the suggestion, you can usually click **Apply Correction** to instantly replace the bad sentence with the good one! ✨
   * If the tool asks for "Human Review," you can type your own corrected sentence into the text box and apply it.

---

## 5. 💾 Exporting Your Work

Once you have gone through the document and fixed the highlighted issues, you will want to save your improved text!

1. Look for the **Export** button at the top right of the Document Content area.
2. Click it to download your newly corrected document to your computer. 🎉

---

## 6. 💡 Tips for Success
* **Take it one step at a time:** If a document has many issues, don't get overwhelmed! Just click through them one by one.
* **You are in control:** You don't *have* to accept every suggestion. If you feel your original sentence is better for your specific audience, you can ignore the warning. 🛡️
* **Use Dark Mode:** If your eyes get tired, toggle the **Dark Mode** 🌙 switch in the top navigation bar for a darker, high-contrast reading experience!

❤️ Happy Writing!
