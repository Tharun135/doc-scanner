🎯 How to Upload

1 Click the button above or visit http://localhost:8000/docs
2 Look for the green "Upload" section in the page
3 Click the "Try it out" button (top right)
4 Click "Choose File" and select your document
5 Click the blue "Execute" button

🔍 After Upload

Your documents will be:

  • ✅ Parsed and chunked automatically
  • ✅ Converted to vector embeddings (384 dimensions)
  • ✅ Stored in ChromaDB for semantic search
  • ✅ Instantly searchable across all documents

⚡ Quick Commands

Upload via PowerShell:

curl.exe -X POST http://localhost:8000/upload -F "file=@your_document.pdf"

Search your documents:

$body = '{"query":"your search","top_k":5}'
curl.exe -X POST http://localhost:8000/query -H "Content-Type: application/json" -d $body
💡 Pro Tip: Upload multiple documents to build a knowledge base. Semantic search will work across ALL uploaded documents simultaneously!
